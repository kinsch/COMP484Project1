Summer 2025

Overview

At a Glance

This summer was a mix of lattes, laptops, and last-minute flights. I spent more time than I should have café hopping with friends, joined the software engineering team of a cruise line at Princess Cruises, and decided to book a flight across the country a week before school started.

It wasn't the most extravagant summer of all time, but my camera roll is full to the brim with more photos I thought my phone had storage for. I put this site together to serve as a little summer journal, and so I hope you enjoy flipping through (clicking through) some of my favorite places and happenings!

Café Hopping

One of my favorite parts of summer is having the free time to go places at a time when I'd usually be in class. These past few months, I've tried a variety of new places- including new foods and drinks! Getting to go out to the places that appear on my brilliantly-curated social media reccomendations is one of the best parts of summer break.

Princess Cruises

I had my first taste of corporate life this Summer. I joined a cruise company- Princess Cruises as a business analyst intern. I worked with cross-functional teams and our stakeholders to create software requirements. In addition to software specifications, I spent a lot of time learning about and working with our change management, QA, DevOps, and XOps teams.

Post this experience, I've returned to the company as a Jr. Business Analyst and do much of the same stuff I learned as an intern! So when I wasn't out with friends or trying new places, I was probably in my cutely decorated cubicle.

Travelling With Friends

The week before school started, I headed to Boston to meet up with my friends! One of my closest friends moved there for the summer to complete her research and so I stayed with her for a bit! While I was there, I caught up with some other friends who'd moved there permanently. We visited so many states in such little time (6 states in 5 days!)
